The Best Movies According to Talaina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avatar

The visual effects are stunning. It also tackles current social issues like racism in a futuristic way.

Pulp Fiction

The wildest story ever told. There are many stories that all tie together, with scenes shot out of chronological order. A true work of art.

The Color Purple

A masterpiece about the struggles of black people in America, with some of the greatest performances from prominent black actors.
